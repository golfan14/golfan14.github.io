About Me

My name is Stephen Ermisch and this is my very first website. I just got into coding a few months ago and I am loving it. Now a little bit about myself. I was born in Upstate New York but I grew up in Colorado. I went to the University of Colorado at Colorado Springs where I earned a degree in Business with a specialization in PGA Golf Management. I worked at seven different golf courses before I got tired of that and decided to change careers. I have worked in finance for the last four years and am once again looking for a change. I got married to my beautiful wife Alison in October 2016 and we are expecting our first child.
